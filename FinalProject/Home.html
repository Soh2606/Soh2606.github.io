Gola

Ice gola is a traditional
Indian shaved ice dessert
from Mumbai. It is also
known as an ice lolly or
snow cone in other parts
of the world. What
differntiates a gola from
other forms of shaved ice
are the flavours and toppings.
The syrups used in India
are made of native fruits
like 'Jamun', 'Chickoo', mango
and more.

Golas are always a delicious treat for both little
monsters, like Mishri, and kids alike!
